The Game 69

A game by Bill Studio

Play with the inverse of 6 and 9. At first glance, this game may seem simple, but it will test your reflexes and focus. The core gameplay is a fast-paced reaction challenge.

How to Play

  • Rule 1 If the text shows '6', tap the '9' button.
  • Rule 2 If the text shows '69', tap the '9' button, then the '6' button.

The game also features a collection of other popular offline games like 2048, Color Switch, and more, all within a single app and you can play it offline.
